# Project Context

## Niche

Indoor anti-sweat sports shoes for fitness studios.

Eco-responsible sneakers specifically designed for indoor training that prevent sweat from passing through the sole and wetting socks and gym mats, while offering breathability, grip, and hygiene.

## Target Audience

Regular gym-goers: cross-training, weightlifting, HIIT, yoga, pilates.

## Problem Statement

- Excessive foot sweating
- Soaked socks
- Wet gym mats → slippery, unhygienic
- Bad odors
- Regular shoes poorly adapted for indoor use

## Solution

Indoor technical shoes combining:

- Anti-transfer moisture sole (sweat doesn't pass through)
- Fast-drying breathable fabric
- Antibacterial anti-odor membrane
- Special gym floor grip
- Recycled / eco-responsible materials

## Unique Selling Proposition

**Positioning:** "The first indoor sneaker designed to keep your feet, socks, and mat perfectly dry."

| Attribute | Customer Benefit |
| :--- | :--- |
| Dry feet | Constant comfort during exercise |
| Clean mat | Safer and more hygienic training |
| Anti-odor | Confidence and freshness |
| Eco-responsible | Purchase aligned with values |
| Fitness design | Modern style, gym & lifestyle |
